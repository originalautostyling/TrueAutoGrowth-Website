Our First Case Study

The first shop we grew with this system was our own.

Where We Started

Good work, around $15k a month, and stuck.

Our shop did work we were proud of. But the months were inconsistent, the growth came and went with referrals, and we were doing all the marketing ourselves in whatever time was left over. The work was never the problem. Everything around the work is what had us stuck.

We knew the install side cold. What we did not have was a system that brought in the right jobs on purpose instead of by luck.

What We Built

So we built the system on ourselves first.

We started with the foundation, the part most shops skip. Then we layered on visibility, lead flow, and follow-up, and we tracked all of it. These are the exact six systems we now build for the shops we partner with.

01

Positioning

We made our shop look and sound like the quality of work we actually delivered, so we stopped attracting price shoppers.

02

Visibility

We got found by the right buyers locally, through Google, Maps, and search, instead of waiting on word of mouth.

03

Lead Flow

We built campaigns around our highest-value services so the inquiries coming in were the ones worth booking.

04

Conversion

We tightened response time and put a real follow-up process behind every lead, so fewer slipped away.

05

Tracking

We tracked every lead to its source and every booked job to revenue, so we always knew what was working.

06

Scale

We used the data to keep doubling down on what produced, and that is what moved the number month over month.

What Happened, And What Still Is

It compounded to $60k a month, and we never turned it off.

It did not come from one viral post or a lucky stretch. It came from a system that kept producing, month after month, until our shop was running at around $60k a month. The same path our Foundation and Market Leader systems are built around.

And here is the part that matters most: we still run these exact systems in our own shop today, as we keep growing. We are not selling you something we tried once and walked away from. We are handing you the playbook we use every single week.
