Paint Protection Film

PPF Shop Marketing

PPF is high-ticket and high-consideration. Buyers research for weeks and compare shops carefully before they spend that kind of money. We build the trust and visibility that move them from researching to booked with you.

  • Rank for the searches serious PPF buyers actually use
  • Position your installs against the cheap, rushed competition

Window Tint

Window Tint Marketing

Tint is competitive with strong, steady local demand. The shops that win are the ones that own their local search results and run campaigns that actually get called back. That is what we build.

  • Own local search and Google Maps for tint in your area
  • Run paid campaigns built for high-intent local buyers
  • Turn quote calls into booked appointments with better follow-up

Keywords we target: window tint shop marketing, window tint SEO, marketing for tint shops

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic Coating Marketing

Ceramic buyers educate themselves before they commit. They read, they compare, they ask about durability and warranties. We build the positioning that puts a serious studio in front of them during that window and earns the trust to win the job.

  • Build authority content that answers what ceramic buyers ask

Vinyl Wrap

Vinyl Wrap Marketing

Wrap buyers value craftsmanship and want to see it. We attract the customer who is ready to invest in a quality color change or finish, not the one shopping the cheapest price per square foot.

  • Position your shop as the premium option, not the bargain one
