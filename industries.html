Paint Protection Film

PPF shops have a trust
and education problem.

Most buyers who are ready to spend on paint protection film are still in research mode when they first find your shop. They are comparing installers, reading reviews, trying to understand the difference between partial and full coverage, and figuring out whether the price is worth it.

If your digital presence does not clearly answer those questions and position your shop as the quality option — they book someone else. Not because that shop is better. Because their marketing infrastructure is.

Window Tint

Tint is competitive.
Visibility is everything.

Window tint is one of the most searched automotive services in any city. That is the opportunity. It is also highly competitive — every market has multiple options and buyers often go with whoever shows up first and has solid reviews.

Winning in tint comes down to local search visibility, GBP optimization, review volume, and fast lead response. A buyer who searches, finds you, and gets a quick response is likely to book. One who waits two days for a reply is gone.
