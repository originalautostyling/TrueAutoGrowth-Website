Window Tint

Tint buyers move fast. Visibility and response speed are everything.

The buyer problem: Window tint is one of the most searched automotive services in any city. That is the opportunity. It is also one of the most competitive because buyers often choose based on who shows up first and has enough reviews to look credible. Decision cycles are short.

A buyer who searches, finds you, and gets a same-day response is very likely to book. One who submits a form and waits two days is gone.

What TAG builds: Local search dominance through GBP optimization and paid search, review volume strategy, fast response infrastructure via CRM, and film-grade clarity so buyers understand exactly what they are getting.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic buyers are informed. Your positioning needs to hold up under scrutiny.

The buyer problem: The ceramic coating buyer is typically more informed than the average automotive service customer. If your content, reviews, and positioning cannot hold up to a buyer who knows what they are looking for, they will choose the shop whose marketing reflects the same level of quality.

What TAG builds: Education-first content that answers research-phase questions, tier clarity so buyers understand your coating options and what each delivers, SEO built around the specific searches ceramic buyers make, and a nurture process for leads with longer decision windows.
