    Market Availability

    Do you work with shops nationwide?
    Yes. We work with qualifying shops across the United States. Where you are located is not the issue. Market availability is.
    How do I find out if my market is open?
    Submit the form on the contact page. We check availability first and respond before we discuss anything else. If the market is taken we tell you up front.

    Market Protection

    Do you work with more than one shop in the same area?
    No. To protect our clients and avoid competing against our own work, we only partner with one qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area. Once a market is claimed it stays closed to competing shops for the life of the partnership.
    What does protected market exclusivity actually mean?
    It means the strategy we build for your shop, the positioning, the campaigns, the messaging stays yours. We are not going to build the same infrastructure for your competitor down the road. Once your market is claimed it is off limits to competing shops.
    What if my market gets claimed while I am thinking about it?
    Markets fill on a first-come basis. If a qualifying shop in your area submits before you do, that market closes. If you are seriously considering reaching out, do not wait.

    Pricing and Contracts

    How is pricing determined?
    Pricing is based on scope of work. What your shop needs, what your current assets look like, how competitive your market is, and which channels are being used all factor in. We do not have flat rates that apply the same way to every shop.
    Are contracts long-term?
    All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on the scope of work and what your shop needs. We do not lock you into multi-year contracts.
    Is ad spend included in pricing?
    No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the advertising platforms and is separate from our fee. We help you determine the right budget for your market on your intake call.
    Do you take performance pay?
    No. We work on a retainer model only. Pricing is a flat monthly fee based on scope of work. There is no performance-based component to our pricing.

    What Is Included

    Do you set up and manage a CRM?
    Yes. CRM setup and implementation is included in every package starting at Foundation. This is one of the most important pieces of the infrastructure and one of the most commonly overlooked.
    Do you help with offer optimization?
    Yes. How your services are packaged, priced, and positioned has a direct impact on lead quality and close rate. We work on offer clarity and positioning as part of every engagement.
    Do you build landing pages?
    Yes. Website and landing page improvements are part of Foundation. Landing page strategy for paid campaigns is included in Market Leader and Local Dominator.
    Do you help with Google Ads?
    Yes. Google Ads strategy and campaign management is included in the Market Leader and Local Dominator packages.
    Do you help with SEO?
    Yes. Local SEO cleanup is part of Foundation. Local SEO and GBP growth is in Market Leader. Advanced SEO and local authority building is in Local Dominator.
    Do you manage social media?
    Content strategy is part of certain packages. We are not a social media management agency. Our focus is on infrastructure that produces booked jobs. Follower counts are not a metric we care about.

    Who Fits

    What type of shops are the right fit?
    PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations. Specifically shops that do quality work and want to build real marketing infrastructure rather than run a few boosted posts and hope something sticks.
    Do you work with newer shops?
    It depends on the situation. Shops that are earlier in their operation typically start with Foundation to get the underlying pieces working properly before scaling paid spend. We will tell you what makes sense on the intake call.
    Do you work with businesses outside of automotive restyling and detailing?
    No. We work exclusively in this space. That focus is intentional and it is what keeps our work relevant and our understanding of the industry sharp.

    Getting Started

    What happens after I submit the form?
    We review your submission, check whether your market is available, and look at your current online presence before we get on a call. When we connect, we walk through what we see, share honest feedback on what we would improve and why, and discuss whether working together actually makes sense for your shop. No pressure. Just a real conversation.
    Do you guarantee results?
    No. We will not promise a specific number of leads or a revenue outcome. Growth depends on your market, your offer, your pricing, and execution on both sides. What we will do is build the best possible infrastructure for your shop and be transparent about what it is producing.
