About TAG

What makes TAG different from a normal marketing agency?
We work exclusively in the automotive restyling and detailing space and bring both marketing strategy and real shop-side experience to every engagement. Our co-founder David Hoggatt has 11 plus years of hands-on experience in PPF, tint, ceramic, and detailing. Co-founder Sean Kennedy has 7 plus years on the marketing side of this industry. Most agencies need the shop owner to explain the services. We already understand them.
Do you only help with ads?
No. Paid ads are one component of the infrastructure. We build GBP optimization, website improvements, tracking setup, CRM implementation, offer clarity, review strategy, local SEO, follow-up processes, landing pages, and content direction. Ads come after the foundation is solid enough to support them properly.
Why does the foundation matter before ad spend?
Because ads send people somewhere. If the landing page is weak, the offer is unclear, tracking is not set up, and there is no follow-up process after the inquiry comes in, you are paying to send traffic into a system that cannot convert it. We fix the foundation first so the ad spend you invest actually produces booked jobs rather than just clicks.

Market and Exclusivity

Why do you only work with one shop per market?
Because the strategy we build for your shop, the positioning, campaigns, messaging, and offer structure, should not be competing against itself. If we worked with two PPF shops in the same city, we would be on both sides. That is not something we are willing to do. One qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area.
What does protected market exclusivity mean in practice?
Once we take on a shop in a market, we will not bring on a competing shop offering the same services in that protected area for the life of the partnership. The strategy we build, the positioning we develop, and the campaigns we run stay yours.
Do you work with shops nationwide?
Yes. Geography is not the limiting factor. Market availability is. Submit the form and we check availability first.

Pricing and Contracts

How is pricing determined?
Pricing is based on scope of work. What your shop needs, what your current assets look like, how competitive your market is, which channels are required, and how much implementation is involved. We do not force every shop into the same plan at the same price.
Are contracts long-term?
All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on the scope of work. We do not lock clients into multi-year contracts.
Is ad spend included in pricing?
No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the advertising platforms and is separate from our management fee. We help you set an appropriate budget for your market during the intake call.
Do you take performance pay?
No. We work on a retainer model only. Our pricing is a flat monthly fee based on scope of work.

What Is Included

Do you help with CRM and follow-up?
Yes. CRM setup and implementation is included in Foundation and every package above it. This is one of the most commonly neglected parts of a shop's operation and one of the highest-leverage things to get right. A lead that does not get a structured follow-up is essentially a lost lead.
Do you build landing pages?
Yes. Website and landing page improvements are part of Foundation. Landing page strategy for paid campaigns is included in Market Leader and Local Dominator.
Do you help with Google Ads?
Yes. Google Ads strategy and management is included in Market Leader and Local Dominator packages.
Do you help with SEO?
Yes. Local SEO cleanup is included in Foundation. Local SEO and GBP growth is in Market Leader. Advanced SEO, local authority building, and content strategy is in Local Dominator.

Who Is a Fit

What type of shops are the right fit?
PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations. Specifically shops that care about the quality of their work, want better clients rather than just more volume, and are willing to build real infrastructure rather than run a few boosted posts.
What kind of shops are not a fit?
Shops looking for the cheapest leads, discount-heavy campaigns, or overnight results. Shops competing entirely on price. Shops that are not willing to invest time in the process. If you are expecting to see a major turnaround in 24 to 48 hours, we are not the right fit and we will tell you that upfront.
Do you work with newer shops?
It depends on the situation. Newer shops typically start with Foundation to get the underlying pieces working before scaling paid spend. We will tell you honestly what makes sense on the intake call.

Getting Started

What happens after I submit the form?
We review your submission, check whether your market is available, and look at your current online presence before we reach out. When we connect, we walk through what we see, give you honest feedback on what we would improve and why, share specific suggestions for what would make an immediate difference in your business, and discuss whether working together makes sense. No pitch. Just a real conversation.
How long does it take to see traction?
It depends on your starting point and package. Paid ads with proper tracking and landing pages can start producing leads relatively quickly once the foundation is correct. SEO, GBP authority, and organic positioning take longer but build compounding visibility that reduces dependency on paid spend over time. We set realistic expectations on the intake call.
Do you guarantee results?
No. We do not make guarantees about specific lead numbers or revenue outcomes. Growth depends on your market, your offer, your pricing, and execution on both sides. Anyone promising overnight results or guaranteed numbers is not giving you an honest answer. What we will do is build the strongest possible infrastructure for your shop and be transparent about what it is producing.
