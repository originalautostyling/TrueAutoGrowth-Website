Market Availability

Do you work with shops nationwide?
Yes. True Auto Growth works with qualifying shops across the United States. Geography is not the limiting factor — market availability is what determines whether we can take on a new client in your area.
How do I know if my market is open?
Submit the form on the contact page. We check availability and respond before we discuss anything else. If the market is taken, we tell you immediately so you are not wasting your time.

Market Protection

Do you work with more than one shop in the same area?
No. To protect our clients and avoid competing against our own strategy, we only partner with one qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area. Once a market is claimed, it is closed to competing shops until the partnership ends.
What does protected market exclusivity mean?
Once we partner with a shop in a market, we will not take on another client offering the same services in that protected area. The positioning we build, the strategy we develop, and the infrastructure we put in place are yours — not shared with a competitor down the road.
What if my market gets claimed while I am deciding?
Markets are claimed on a first-application basis. If a qualifying shop in your area submits before you do, that market closes. If you are seriously considering reaching out, sooner is better.

Pricing & Scope

How is pricing determined?
Pricing is based on scope of work. What channels are needed, what your current assets look like, how competitive your market is, and how much implementation is required all factor in. We do not force every shop into the same plan. Starting points are discussed on your intake call.
Is ad spend included in the pricing?
No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the advertising platforms — separate from our fee. Our pricing covers strategy, campaign management, landing pages, SEO, and implementation. We help you determine the right budget for your market during the intake call.
Are there long-term contracts?
Our agreements are structured to give both sides a fair runway to build and see results, without locking you into something unreasonable. Specifics are covered on your intake call.

What's Included

Do you build landing pages?
Yes. Landing page strategy is included in the Market Leader and Local Dominator packages. Website and landing page improvements are also part of the Foundation package.
Do you help with Google Ads?
Yes. Google Ads strategy and campaign management is included in the Market Leader and Local Dominator packages.
Do you help with SEO?
Yes. Local SEO cleanup is included in the Foundation package. Local SEO and GBP growth is in the Market Leader. Full SEO and local authority building is in the Local Dominator.
Do you manage social media?
Content strategy is part of certain packages, but we are not a social media management agency. Our focus is on building the structure that produces booked jobs — not follower counts.

Who's a Fit

What type of shops are a good fit?
PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations — particularly shops that do quality work and want real structure behind the business, not just a few boosted posts.
Do you work with newer shops?
It depends on the situation. Shops that are newer and still building their operation typically start with the Foundation package to get the right pieces in place before scaling paid spend. We will tell you honestly what makes sense on the intake call.
Do you work with businesses outside of automotive restyling and detailing?
No. We work exclusively with PPF, tint, ceramic, wrap, and detailing businesses. That focus is intentional.

Getting Started

What happens after I submit the form?
We review your information and check whether your market is available. If it is open and your shop looks like a potential fit, we reach out to schedule an intake call. On that call we look at your current setup, your market, and talk through which package makes the most sense to start with.
Do you guarantee results?
No. We will not promise a specific number of leads or revenue outcome. Growth depends on your market, your pricing, your offer, and execution on both sides. What we will do is build the strongest possible structure for your shop and be transparent about what it is producing.
