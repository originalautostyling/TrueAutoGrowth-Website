Market and Exclusivity

Why do you only work with one shop per market?
Because we cannot build a competitive positioning system for your shop and then build the same thing for the shop down the street. The strategy we develop, the campaigns we manage, and the infrastructure we build are yours within your protected service area. That exclusivity is how we protect the value of what we do for every client.
How do I find out if my market is available?
Submit the form on the contact page. We check availability before discussing anything else. If the market is taken, we tell you right away so you are not wasting time.
What happens if someone takes my market while I am deciding?
Markets are claimed on a first-come basis. If a qualifying shop in your area submits before you do, that market closes. If you are seriously considering reaching out, waiting does not work in your favor.

What Makes TAG Different

What makes TAG different from a normal marketing agency?
TAG combines 7 years of automotive marketing experience with 11 years of real shop-side experience in PPF, tint, ceramic, wrap, and detailing. We are built exclusively for this industry. We understand the buyer, the service, the shop operations, and the lead quality problem in a way a generalist agency cannot replicate. We also work with one shop per market, which means we are not competing against our own strategy.
Do you only help with ads?
No. Paid ads are one piece of a larger system. We also build websites and landing pages, Google Business Profile, local SEO, CRM and follow-up, offer optimization, review growth strategy, lead tracking, and content direction. The goal is to build an infrastructure where all of those pieces are connected rather than running independently.

Pricing and Contracts

How is pricing determined?
Pricing is based on scope of work. What channels are needed, what your current assets look like, how competitive your market is, and how much implementation is required all factor in. We do not force every shop into the same plan. Starting price points are listed on the packages page. Final pricing is discussed on the intake call.
Are contracts long-term?
All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on scope and what your shop needs. We do not lock you into multi-year contracts.
Do you take performance pay?
No. We work on a retainer model only. A flat monthly fee based on scope of work. There is no performance-based component to our pricing.
Is ad spend included in the pricing?
No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the advertising platforms, separate from our fee. We help you determine the right budget for your market on the intake call.

What Is Included

Do you help with CRM and follow-up?
Yes. CRM setup and implementation is included in every package starting at Foundation. Follow-up process improvements are part of every engagement. This is one of the most important and most commonly overlooked pieces, and we treat it accordingly.
Do you build landing pages?
Yes. Website and landing page improvements are part of Foundation. Conversion-focused landing pages for paid campaigns are included in Market Leader and Local Dominator.
Do you help with Google Ads?
Yes. Google Ads strategy and campaign management is included in the Market Leader and Local Dominator packages.
Do you help with SEO?
Yes. Local SEO cleanup is included in Foundation. Local SEO and GBP growth is in Market Leader. Advanced SEO and local authority building is in Local Dominator. We treat SEO as infrastructure that reduces dependency on paid ads over time, not an afterthought.

Results and Timeline

Do you guarantee results?
No. We will not promise specific lead numbers or revenue outcomes. Growth depends on your market, your offer, your pricing, your shop's response time, and execution on both sides. What we will do is build the best possible infrastructure for your shop and be transparent about what it is producing. Anyone guaranteeing specific results on a fixed timeline is not being straight with you.
How long does it take to see traction?
It depends on what is being built. Paid campaigns can produce leads relatively quickly once the foundation is in place. Organic SEO, local authority, and review volume take longer and build over time. Foundational infrastructure typically takes 60 to 90 days to be fully set up and running. Anyone promising meaningful results in 24 to 48 hours is not being honest.
Why does the foundation need to come before ad spend?
Running paid traffic to a weak landing page with no tracking, no CRM, and no follow-up process is expensive and produces bad data. The foundation ensures every dollar spent on acquisition has the best chance of producing a booked job you can actually trace back to the source.

Who Is a Fit

What kind of shops are the right fit?
PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations. Specifically shops that do quality work and are serious about building a real structure behind the business rather than chasing a quick fix.
Do you work with newer shops?
It depends on the situation. Shops that are earlier in their operation typically start with the Foundation package to get the underlying pieces working properly before scaling paid acquisition. We will be direct about what makes sense on the intake call.
What kind of shops are not a fit?
Shops looking for the cheapest leads, discount campaigns, or quick fixes that promise results overnight are not the right fit. We are built for shops that care about quality, want better clients, and understand that sustainable growth takes structure and time.
