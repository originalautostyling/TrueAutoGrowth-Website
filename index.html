The Real Problem

Good shops get overlooked because their marketing does not match the quality of their work.

Most shop owners are busy doing the work. Managing jobs, training installers, handling customers, ordering materials. The pipeline, CRM, tracking, reviews, SEO, and follow-up get pushed to the back burner because there is no time and no system to run them properly.

Many quality shops reach a point where word of mouth gets them to a comfortable level, then growth stalls. Not because the work is not there. Because the structure is not there.

That is what we fix. Before running more ads. Before posting more content. We build the infrastructure that makes everything else work better.

FAQ

Common questions.

Why do you only work with one shop per market?
Because the strategy we build for your shop, the positioning, campaigns, and messaging, should not be working against you. If we took on a competing shop in your area, we would be on both sides. That is not how we operate. One qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area.
What makes TAG different from a normal marketing agency?
Most agencies bring a campaign template and apply it to whatever business hires them. We work exclusively in the automotive restyling and detailing space and bring both marketing strategy and real shop-side experience to every engagement. We know the services, the buyer psychology, the sales cycle, and where most shops lose leads in day-to-day operations.
Do you guarantee results?
No. We do not make guarantees because growth depends on your market, your pricing, your offer, and execution on both sides. Anyone who promises you a specific lead number or revenue outcome in 24 to 48 hours is selling you something we would not buy. What we will do is build the strongest possible infrastructure for your shop and be transparent about what it is producing.
How long does it take to see traction?
It depends on the starting point and which package makes sense for your shop. Paid ads can produce results relatively quickly once the tracking and landing page are correct. SEO, GBP authority, and organic positioning take longer but create more sustainable, lower-cost traffic over time. We set realistic expectations on the intake call.
Are contracts long-term?
All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on the scope of work. We do not lock you into multi-year contracts.
