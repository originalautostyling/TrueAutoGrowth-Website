Who We Are

Building a marketing infrastructure for a PPF shop is not the same as building one for a restaurant or a law firm. The buyer is different. The trust threshold is different. The sales cycle is different. The objections are different. We know this firsthand.

We build the foundation, visibility, tracking, and follow-up that helps serious shops turn more attention into booked work.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with shops nationwide?
Yes. True Auto Growth works with qualifying shops across the United States. Geography is not a limitation — market availability is what determines whether we can take on a new client in your area.
Do you work with more than one shop in the same area?
No. To protect our clients and avoid competing against our own strategy, we only partner with one qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area. Once a market is taken, it is closed to competing shops until the partnership ends.
Is ad spend included in pricing?
No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the advertising platforms — separate from our fee. Our pricing covers strategy, management, and implementation.
How is pricing determined?
Pricing is based on scope of work — what is needed, what your current assets look like, and what your market requires. We do not force every shop into the same plan.
What type of shops are a good fit?
PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations — shops that do quality work and want to build a real structure behind the business.
