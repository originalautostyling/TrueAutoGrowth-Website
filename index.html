FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with shops nationwide?
Yes. We work with qualifying shops across the United States. Where you are located is not the limiting factor. Market availability is what determines whether we can take on a new client in your area.
Why do you only work with one shop per market?
Because we cannot build a competitive positioning strategy for your shop and then build the same thing for the shop down the street. The strategy we develop, the campaigns we run, and the infrastructure we build are yours within your protected service area. That is how we protect the value of what we do for you.
Are contracts long-term?
All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on scope and what your shop needs. We do not lock you into multi-year contracts.
How long does it take to see traction?
It depends on what is being built. Paid campaigns can produce leads relatively quickly once the foundation is in place. Organic SEO, local authority, and review growth take longer but build something that compounds over time. Anyone telling you there are meaningful results in 24 to 48 hours is not being straight with you.
What kind of shops are not a fit?
Shops looking for the cheapest lead volume, quick fixes, or discount-heavy campaigns are not the right fit. We work with shops that care about quality, want the right clients, and are willing to build the structure required to grow consistently.
