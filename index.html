FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with shops nationwide?
Yes. We work with qualifying shops across the United States. Where you are located is not the limiting factor. Market availability is.
Do you work with more than one shop in the same area?
No. To protect our clients and avoid competing against our own strategy, we only partner with one qualifying shop per protected 50-mile service area. Once a market is taken it is closed to competing shops until the partnership ends.
Are contracts long-term?
All agreements are month to month. The length of the engagement depends on the scope of work and what your shop needs. We do not lock you into multi-year contracts.
Is ad spend included in pricing?
No. Ad spend for Google Ads and Meta Ads is paid directly to the platforms, separate from our fee. Our pricing covers strategy, management, and implementation.
What kind of shops are the right fit?
PPF installers, window tint shops, ceramic coating studios, vinyl wrap businesses, and auto detailing operations. Specifically shops that do quality work and are ready to build real infrastructure behind the business rather than run a few ads and hope something sticks.
