Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This policy is provided for general transparency. It is a starting template and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney to ensure it fits your specific business and complies with the laws that apply to you.

True Auto Growth ("True Auto Growth," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website at trueautogrowth.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit our market availability or contact form. This may include your name, email address, phone number, shop name, website, location, and details about your business that you choose to share.

We also automatically collect limited technical information when you visit the site, such as your browser type, device, pages viewed, and general location derived from your IP address, typically through standard analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and determine market availability and fit.
  • To communicate with you about our services.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To understand how visitors use the site through aggregated analytics.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers who help us operate our business, such as our CRM, email, hosting, and analytics providers, and only to the extent needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & Analytics

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and measure site performance. Most browsers let you control or disable cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site function.

Data Retention

We keep the information you provide for as long as needed to respond to you, provide our services, and meet legal or business requirements. You may request that we delete your information by contacting us.

Your Choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have provided by emailing us at the address below. You can also opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in those messages.

Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites or services that we do not control. This policy does not apply to those sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business owners and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at sales@trueautogrowth.com.
