Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact or availability form on this site, we collect information you provide including your name, email address, phone number, shop name, city and state, and information about your business. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and determine whether your market is available.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to contact you, evaluate whether a partnership makes sense, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not share your information with anyone outside of True Auto Growth without your consent, except as required by law.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our pages. These tools may use cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Analytics data is aggregated and not tied to personally identifiable information.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect information you submit. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure. By submitting information through this site, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is used, contact us at sales@trueautogrowth.com.
