Why We Built This

Good shops were getting overlooked because their marketing did not match the quality of their work.

There are a lot of shops in this industry doing genuinely excellent work. Clean installs. Careful prep. Proper education for the customer. Real craftsmanship. But the marketing around those shops often tells a completely different story or no story at all.

Shop owners are busy. They are managing jobs, handling customers, doing the actual work. The pipeline, the CRM, the tracking, the reviews, the follow-up, the SEO, the offer structure. These things get neglected because there is not enough time or bandwidth to do them right while also running the operation.

That is the gap we built TAG to close. Not by selling more ads. By building the infrastructure that connects the quality of your work to the type of customer who is actually looking for it.

The Team

Two founders. Two sides of the same industry.

Sean Kennedy
Founder · Marketing Strategy

The strategy side.

Sean has spent over 7 years building marketing infrastructure for businesses in the automotive restyling and detailing space. His work spans positioning, paid search, paid social, SEO, landing pages, content, tracking, CRM, and follow-up systems.

What separates his approach is understanding that the marketing strategy has to fit the specific buying behavior of someone shopping for PPF, ceramic coating, or a full vinyl wrap. What We Believe

The principles behind how we work.

01

Good shops should not compete only on price.

If your marketing cannot clearly communicate the difference between a proper install and a discount one, you will always be competing at the bottom. That is a positioning problem, not a quality problem.

02

Foundation before ad spend.

Putting more money into ads before your tracking, offer, landing pages, and CRM are solid is expensive and frustrating. We start with the infrastructure.

03

A lead is only valuable if it gets followed up with.

The shop that closes high-ticket work is not always the best installer. It is often the one with the best follow-up process. We build that process into the foundation.

04

Organic authority reduces dependency on paid ads.

The goal is to build SEO, reviews, and local presence strong enough that your shop is visible without having to pay for every click. Paid ads accelerate. Organic compounds.

05

Growth requires patience and consistent execution.

Anyone selling 24 to 48 hour results is not being honest with you. Real growth takes structure, time, and consistent work across multiple channels. We will tell you that upfront.

06

One market. One partner.

We do not build a competitive positioning system for your shop and then turn around and offer the same thing to your direct competitor. Protected market exclusivity is how we operate.

Our Mission

To help premium PPF, tint, ceramic, wrap, and detailing shops become the obvious choice in their market.

Not through shortcuts. Not through guaranteed claims we cannot back up. Through building the foundation, visibility, tracking, and follow-up that makes the quality of your work easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to book.
