Why It Matters

The difference between knowing the industry and being in it.

Most agencies that claim to specialize in automotive have run some ads for a tint shop once. They have never quoted a full PPF wrap. They cannot explain why a ceramic coating buyer thinks differently than someone getting a detail. They do not know what DYNOshield is or why install environment matters for film longevity.

We are different because one of our owners is actively embedded in the automotive restyling space. That proximity is not a marketing angle — it directly shapes how we build the strategy, write the messaging, structure the offers, and identify where shops are losing leads and revenue.

When we build something for your shop, it is built by people who understand your services, your customer, and what actually drives a serious buyer to choose you over the shop down the street.

How We Think

What guides every decision we make.

Foundation Before Spend

Putting more money into ads before the foundation is solid rarely works. Tracking, landing pages, offer clarity, and follow-up need to be right first. We start there.

Structure Over Campaigns

A campaign runs and stops. The right structure compounds over time — visibility, trust, tracking, and follow-up that keeps working after the initial build.

One Market. One Partner.

We will not build a marketing infrastructure for your shop and then build one for your direct competitor. Protected market exclusivity is how we operate — always.

This Industry Only

We work exclusively with PPF, tint, ceramic, wrap, and detailing businesses. No generalist clients. That focus keeps our strategy sharp and directly applicable to the shops we serve.

Honest About Results

We do not promise specific lead numbers or revenue outcomes. Growth depends on your market, your pricing, and execution on both sides. We will tell you what we see — straight.

Visible Attribution

You should know what is working. We set up tracking from the start so you can see which channels and pages are producing real jobs — not just impressions and clicks.
