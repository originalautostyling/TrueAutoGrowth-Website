    Why the Pieces Matter Together

    One channel does not win the market.
    All of them together does.

    Paid captures demand

    Google Ads put your shop in front of buyers who are actively searching right now. Meta Ads build awareness and bring in buyers before they are even ready to search. Both matter.

    SEO owns the market long-term

    Ranking in the top three for your city and your services is the most sustainable source of qualified leads. It takes time to build, which is exactly why you start it now.

    CRM and follow-up closes what ads generate

    Paid ads bring leads in. Without a proper CRM and follow-up process, a large percentage of those leads go cold before anyone closes them. Both sides have to be working.
